After start of the game You will be asked to place your
ships on the grid, there are 3 ships to be placed:
1x Bttleship and 2x Destroyers. In order to place a ship
You should first decide its orientation (type 'v' for 
vertical or 'h' for horizontal) and confirm your choice
by pressing ENTER and then specify coordinates where it
will land. As soon as You will place all of your
ships the fun begins, it's time to pick target and fire
your shot. This is done by entering coordinates of
the form “A5”, where “A” is the column and “5” is the row,
and confirming by pressing ENTER.
Each shot will be met with an answer of miss/hit/sunk.
The game ends when all the ships of one of the players 
get sunk. Good luck.
