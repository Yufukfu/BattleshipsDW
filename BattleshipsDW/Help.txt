                @@ BATTLESHIPS HELP @@
This is a one-sided game of Battleships against ships
placed by the computer. The Game consist of 2 main phases.
First one is placing all of the ships on the grid and
the second one is firing shots at the enemy fleet.

## SHIP PLACEMENT

In order to place a ship first select its orientation
'v' for vertical or 'h' for horizontal + ENTER,
then enter coordinates in the form of A5 + ENTER.

EXAMPLE:
Placing Battleship, length of this ship is 5.
Choose ship orientation (h - horizontal, v - vertical):
h
Input coordinates e.g. B2 or G3 :
C3
